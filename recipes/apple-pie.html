Mini Apple Pie

Description

This is a recipe originally coming from Nino's home on Youtube (hence the photo above coming from his channel). I've used this for my economics class and it turned out great!
I liked how it turned out and tasted, so here I am sharing it!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tsp butter
  • 2 1/2 Cups chopped apple
  • 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Cinnamon
  • Juice of 1/2 Lemon
  • 1/2 Tsp Cornstarch mixed with 1 Tbsp Water
  • Store bought dough
