Choco Chip Cookies

Description

This is a recipe originally coming from Nino's home on Youtube (hence the photo above coming from his channel). I've used this for my economics class and it turned out great!
I liked how it turned out and tasted, so here I am sharing it!

Ingredients

  • 120g Unsalted butter (無塩バター)
  • 50g Sugar (グラニュー糖)
  • brown sugar (ブラウンシュガー)
  • 1Tsp Vanilla extract (バニラエッセンス)
  • 1 Egg (卵)
  • 150g All purpose flour (中力粉)
  • 1/2Tsp Baking soda (ベーキングソーダ)
  • 50g Dark chocolate (ダークチョコレート)
  • 100g Chocolate chip (チョコチップ)

Steps

  1. Whisk 120 grams of room temperature butter (Feel free to use salted or unsalted). Then add in 50grams of sugar
  2. Add in 60 grams of brown sugar. Continue to whisk until you reach a brown color.
  3. Pour 1 tsp of Vanilla extract and the egg. Whisk again until you reach a light brown color
  4. Add the flour and baking soda into the mixture. Make sure to shift both Ingredients first before doing so.
  5. Start kneading the mixture with a spatula the dough is smooth.
  6. Break the dark chocolate into medium pieces and add it into the dough. Afterwards, add the chocolate chocolate chips and mix.
  7. Gather up some of the mixture into your hand, roll it into a small ball, and place it on the baking tray.
  8. Do this again and again until you've used up all of the mixture and/or lost space in the tray.
  9. Bake at Bake at 190℃ for 12-15 minutes
