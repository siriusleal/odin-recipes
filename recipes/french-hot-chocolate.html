French Hot Chocolate

Description

One of my favorite drinks! I like having this during special occassions. Hope you enjoy :D

Ingredients

  • 113g Milk Chocolate(チョコレート 113g)
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Milk (牛乳 1 1/2 カップ)
  • 1/2 Cup of Heavy Cream (生クリーム 1/2カップ)
  • 1/4 tsp Vanilla (バニラ 小さじ1/4)
  • Sugar (Optional)
  • Honey (Optional)

Steps

  1. Chop the chocolate into small pieces
  2. Pour your Milk and Heavy Cream into a pot. Heat it up
  3. Add in the chocolates and mix until it dissolves. Afterwards, sweeten it according to your tastes with either sugar or honey.
  4. Add in your vanilla and mix.
  5. Pour it all in a mug.

    6. and you're done! that's all! Feel free to add in some whipped cream on top or none at all. :D
